Clustering: The Good, The Bad and The Beautiful

One of the most widely used and most frequently misapplied techniques in machine learning — and how a modern workflow turns messy, high-dimensional data into useful, explainable insights.

The title is a nod to The Bad and the Beautiful (1952), so the running dataset is the top 5,000 movies by TMDB vote count — overviews for text clustering, posters for image clustering.

01 · The Bad

Failure modes that bite every clustering project: clusters from structureless data, instability across runs, raw high-dim distances, and parameter sensitivity masquerading as signal.
